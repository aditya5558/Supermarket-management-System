# Supermarket-management-System
Easily automates the management of a supermarket

Computer Science project made in class 12 - C++

# Abstract
The Project deals with the automation of supermarket.This software will help salespersons in managing the various types of Records pertaining to his/her customer. The salespersons have been recording the customer information in the past and even in the present through their personal manual efforts. And indeed, it consumes their considerable time and energy that could be utilized in the better productive activities. Apart from that, with increasing customer strength, the task of managing information of each individual customer is indeed a cumbersome task.

On the other hand, there are many inherent problems that exist in any manual system , usually, lack of efficiency. Less efficiency has a great impact on the productivity.The project is developed with the objective of making the system reliable, easier, fast, and more informative.
